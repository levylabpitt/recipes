## Tips and Tricks

*Share your experience and institutional knowledge here*

- **For cross-sections**: Use Pt deposition to protect surface before milling
- **Ion beam induced damage**: Lower kV for final polishing (5 kV)
- **Charging**: Use low kV SEM or coat non-conductive samples
- **Drift**: Allow sample to stabilize thermally before high-res imaging
- **TEM prep**: Final polish at 2-5 kV to minimize damage
